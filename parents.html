Parents

Why Should your Child Apply?

STEM Academy will give your child many oppurtunites that not many other schools or programs can give them. It will put a challenge to your child, and their academic lives. They also have mentors and teachers that will help them and be there for them through the whole experience.

Courses your Child will Take

Your child will be taking advanced classes, and even some college courses. In their freshman year, they will be taking, Algebra II Honors and Geometry Honors. They also will be taking classes that no other students take, such as, Intro To Engineering Honors.

Is This the Program for your Child?

Does your child want a bit of a challenge? Is your child willing to work extra hard? Does your child care for amazing opportunities? If you answer yes to any of these questions then we believe this progra is the right one for your child.

Application Process

To apply your child has to write a 300 word essay and submit it by the deadline. They should also have 2 letters of recommendation. Lastly, they would have to take a test that will challenge their minds, to see if they are fit for the program.
