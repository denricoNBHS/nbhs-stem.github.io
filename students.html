Students

What To Expect

STEM is a very rigorous program that offers students an opportunity to take advanced classes that not everyone in their grade level are exposed to. These classes are fast pace which will allow the student to get a feel for what to expect later on in their career or what they think of doing in the future.

The classes you would be expected to take as a freshman STEM academy student are biology honors, algebra 2 honors, geometry honors and introduction to engineering. It is also recommended for the student to have been in the math honors program . If not, then the student will be required to take algebra 1 classes over the summer. Students will have a 1-10 schedule meaning that their first period class will commence at 7:40 and will be able to leave the school at 3:07. The students will move as a unit through their highschool career.
