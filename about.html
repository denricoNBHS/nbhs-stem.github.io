About

When was S.T.E.M. founded?

At North Bergen High School, we have founded our prestigious S.T.E.M. Academy on the 2014-2015 school year where our very first Cohort are now juniors and we are now currently placed with 3 Cohorts!

Our Purpose

The purpose of our program is to prepare our students for a future in S.T.E.M. with an exposure to all the different fields as well as the skills to be successful. Students who complete the 4-year program will not just receive credit for one of the most rigorous cirriculums out there, but will have experiences and oppurtunities that will allow them to differentiate from their peers.

Our Program Goal

The goal of the program is to offer an accelerated and demanding curriculum for our most gifted and talented students who are considering attending highly competitive colleges and universities. The Program will also give the students exposure to many different fields, using a variety of educational expediencies, to find their ideal niche. This will eventually lead to the students entering higher education with a sense of confidence in their future studies.
